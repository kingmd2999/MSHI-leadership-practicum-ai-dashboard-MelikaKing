## De-identification / simulation notes
- This dataset contains no protected health information (PHI).
- No patient names, MRNs, addresses, full DOBs, or other direct identifiers are included.
- Data is used strictly for coursework and portfolio demonstration.

## Intended use in this project
The dataset will be used to explore workflow patterns (e.g., attendance/no-shows, service lines,
communication channels, and wait/service times) and generate leadership-focused improvement ideas.
